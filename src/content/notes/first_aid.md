A list of what I put in my first aid kit I carry with me

### small kit (plastic sleeve)

- bandages
- steri strips
- antibiotic ointment
- alchocol wipe (or iodine wipe, as its less irritating but need to watch for allergies)
- hydrocortisone ointment (irritations/skin)
- burn cream
- meds
  - aspirin
  - benadryl
  - immodium

### elsewhere in my bag

- breathing barrier (CPR)
- nonlatex gloves

### in sewing kit:

- needle

### in edc:

- tweezers
- electrolytes
- lighter for sterilization

### on swiss army knife

- scissors

### in large kit:

- absorbent compress dressings
- gauze roll bandage
- sterile gauze pads
- triangular bandages
